Stretch marks correction

nichename March 11, 2021

 

Stretching is a general term that characterizes the external appearance of the skin. There are two main kind of stretch marks: red and white, depending on the degree of "maturity", which requires different approaches in their correction.

Our posibilities

To correct stretch marks we use:

  • laser treatments:
    • fractional skin rejuvenation - corrects atrophic scars of stretch marks
    • BBL photorejuvenation system help decrease redness apperance
  • RF Lifting for Stretch Marks - Reduces skin laxity and excess of skin
  • injection techniques - nourish the skin, restoring its elasticity
  • cosmetic programs and care.

The fractional laser will affect several "weaknesses" of stretch marks at once. Laser removes microscopic areas of the skin to replace the young cells, which smooth the skin's microrelief, restores moisture processes in the skin. In result, the skin apper a uniform tone, disappear signs of dryness of the epidermis. The deep thermal effect in the dermis activates the natural repair processes in the skin. This will trigger the mechanisms of synthesis of new collagen in the skin. The skin will become more elastic over time, smoothing the edges between the affected and healthy skin areas. Therefore, fractional laser rejuvenation has a more powerful and overall effect for stretch marks. The procedures are carried out with a course of 3-4 procedures with an interval of 1 month. Good and stable treatment results can be achieved by combining fractional rejuvenation with mesotherapy of stretch marks. The management of mesotherapeutic solutions can enhance the synthesis of collagen initiated by the fractional laser. This will provide a more consistent clinical outcome.
